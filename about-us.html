THE ARTIST

Ejiro Omenasa


Ejiro Omenasa


Digital Collage Artist


A digital Collage Artist with a main focus on Afro futurism.

The Nigerian digital collage artist Omenasa’s distinctive personal style brings joy, vibrance and peace.
Omenasa continuously brings attention to West and East African culture and heritage, while predominantly making reference to the beauty of the African woman.
Furthermore, with their surrealistic feels, Omenasa’s pieces are largely considered to be Afro-Futuristic.
It is of our opinion that Omenasa will grow to be one of the most recognised digital artist to come out of Nigeria, if not West Africa.
